Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 31, 2024

At Arkavo, we are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled with the utmost care. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your information in compliance with applicable laws.

Personal Information:

  • Apple Account: Your personal information remains exclusively with your Apple account and is not shared with Arkavo. We do not access or store any personal information associated with your Apple account unless you choose to utilize our optional Arkavo account recovery service.
  • Arkavo Account Recovery Service: If you opt for Arkavo's account recovery service, we will collect and store your personal information in an encrypted form. This service is designed to assist you in regaining access to your Arkavo account in case of loss or other issues. The information collected for this service is encrypted and handled with strict confidentiality.

Usage Data:

  • Data Encryption: All usage data collected by Arkavo is fully encrypted to protect your privacy. This data is used solely to enhance your experience on the platform.
  • Anonymized Summary Data: We collect anonymized, aggregated usage data to analyze trends, such as the most popular features on Arkavo. This summary data does not identify individual users and is used to improve our services.
  • Court-Ordered Disclosures: In the event of a vetted legal request, such as a court order, Arkavo may be required to provide usage data for a specific user and timeframe. This data will only be disclosed as mandated by law and is not associated with Apple.

How We Use Your Information:

  • Service Provision: Your information is used to facilitate account management, provide customer support, and improve your experience on the platform.
  • Legal Compliance: We may use your information to comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms and conditions, and protect the rights of our users.

Data Protection:

  • We employ industry-leading security measures to ensure that your data is protected from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
  • Arkavo does not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or any other purposes not explicitly stated in this policy.

Your Rights:

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access and correct your personal information at any time, subject to the terms of service and legal requirements.
  • Data Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information, provided it is not required for ongoing legal or contractual obligations.

For further details or inquiries regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at legal@arkavo.com.
